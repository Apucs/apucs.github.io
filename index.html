Apu Chandraw Shill

NLP Engineer at Verbex AI · B.Sc. in CSE, RUET

I am an NLP Engineer at Verbex AI, where I conduct innovative research in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), multi-agent systems, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation, with a focus on building AI-powered voice agents.

I graduated from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET) with a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering, ranking 9th out of 131 students (CGPA: 3.57/4.00). My research interests span artificial intelligence, with particular focus on:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Natural Language Processing
  • LLM Security & Generative AI
  • Computer Vision
  • Object Detection & Face Recognition
  • Deep Learning

I am passionate about research, innovative ideas, and developing AI solutions that have real-world impact. You can read more about my research vision in my research statement.
