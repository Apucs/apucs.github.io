About Me

I am an NLP Engineer at Verbex AI, where I contribute to innovative projects in natural language processing, leveraging expertise in deep learning and computer vision. My work focuses on developing LLM-based solutions, including expressive semantic translation systems, multi-agent frameworks, and sophisticated RAG pipelines.

I graduated from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET) with a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering, ranking 9th out of 131 students (CGPA: 3.57/4.00). With a commitment to continuous learning, I have completed multiple nanodegrees from Udacity in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Data Science.

My research interests span artificial intelligence, with particular focus on large language models, their applications, and security considerations. I am passionate about developing AI solutions that have real-world impact.
